NLS

The National Lifeguard Society is committed to promoting water safety and excellence in lifeguarding across Canada. They provide training and resources to lifeguards, ensuring they are well-equipped to prevent and respond to aquatic emergencies effectively.

Red Cross

The Canadian Red Cross is a leading humanitarian organization that provides essential training in first aid, CPR, and emergency preparedness across the country. Their programs empower individuals to respond effectively to emergencies and promote safety in communities.

Importance

Water safety is crucial, especially as warmer weather invites us to pools, lakes, and beaches. Every year, countless lives are lost due to drowning, making it vital to prioritize safety around water. Understanding the risks and taking precautions can be lifesaving for you and your loved ones. Stay informed, stay vigilant, and make water safety a priority!
